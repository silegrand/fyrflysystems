# Martyn's Law Compliance for Schools

**Legislation:** Terrorism (Protection of Premises) Act 2025
**Enforcement date:** April 2027
**Source:** Fyrfly Systems Ltd — [www.fyrflysystems.com/invisible-shield.html](https://www.fyrflysystems.com/invisible-shield.html)

---

## Are Schools in Scope?

Any school whose main hall, gymnasium, or outdoor grounds can accommodate 100 or more people simultaneously is almost certainly a Standard Tier venue. Secondary schools, larger primaries, and any school hosting community events are in scope. Capacity is assessed on the maximum number of persons that could be present simultaneously, not average occupancy.

## Tier Requirements

### Standard Tier (100–799 capacity)
- Designate a responsible person (typically headteacher or nominated deputy)
- Document written emergency procedures
- Train all staff who may be present during an event
- Maintain ongoing monitoring of protective measures
- Procedures must reflect actual technical capability of installed systems

### Enhanced Tier (800+ capacity)
- All Standard Tier requirements, plus:
- Proportionate physical security measures
- Suitably qualified security professional engaged in planning
- Detailed documented plans

## Temporary Events

Martyn's Law applies to events as well as permanent venues. A school fete or community event that can accommodate 100+ people simultaneously triggers Standard Tier duties. [Fyrfly Solar CCTV Towers](../technical/solar-tower-specs.md) provide Martyn's Law-compliant monitoring for temporary events without permanent infrastructure.
