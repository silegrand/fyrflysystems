## What the Assessment Covers

A Fyrfly campus infrastructure assessment is a half-day on-site visit producing a written report within one week.

### CCTV Coverage Audit
- Identification of blind spots and legacy equipment
- Network dependency risk assessment
- AI analytics upgrade pathway

### Access Control Review
- Entry point assessment against KCSiE visitor management requirements
- Lockdown capability evaluation
- Martyn's Law tier determination

### Network Infrastructure Assessment
- Security VLAN separation from learning network
- Bandwidth analysis and resilience testing
- Failover capability review

### Martyn's Law Gap Analysis
- Written assessment against Standard or Enhanced Tier requirements
- Prioritised compliance roadmap with April 2027 enforcement date context

### Written Report
- Prioritised, costed recommendations
- Written for headteachers, business managers, and governing bodies
- Suitable for sharing with MAT board, local authority, or DfE

## Who Should Request an Assessment

- Headteachers and deputies responsible for site safeguarding
- School Business Managers responsible for capital planning
- MAT Estate Directors assessing provision across multiple schools
- DSLs with concerns about physical safeguarding infrastructure

## No-Obligation Terms

The assessment report belongs to the school. Fyrfly does not require a commitment to proceed. Available for single schools and MAT estate teams assessing multiple sites.
